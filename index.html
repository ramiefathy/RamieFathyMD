Welcome! I am currently a PGY-3 Dermatology Resident Physician at Johns Hopkins University. Originally from Knoxville, TN, I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Princeton University (Molecular Biology) and earned my MD from the Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania, where I was inducted into AOA. This site highlights my clinical work, research, leadership, writing, and other interests.

Clinical Experience & Interests

Dermatology Residency (PGY-3)

I am currently completing my dermatology residency training (2023-Present) at Johns Hopkins Hospital, focusing on developing expertise in diagnosing and managing a wide range of skin conditions. My preliminary internship year (2022-2023) was completed at Pennsylvania Hospital.

Clinical Interests

My interests within dermatology include medical dermatology, epidemiology, the impact of environmental factors like climate change on skin health, teledermatology, applications of AI in clinical practice, and exploring integrative approaches to patient care. I am an active member of the Rheumatology-Dermatology Society, Medical Dermatology Society, and Society of Dermatology Hospitalists.

Research Experience

Current Research (Johns Hopkins / Glass Health)

  • Spearheading studies on Large Language Model (LLM) applications in dermatology (dermoscopic analysis, chatbot accuracy, board question generation) as an AI Clinical Fellow at Glass Health.
  • Investigating 3D teledermatology feasibility using specialized cameras and mixed reality headsets.
  • Evaluating the impact of race/ethnicity on dermatomyositis-associated cancer screening.
  • Assessing the effect of GLP-1 agonists on psoriasis and hidradenitis suppurativa severity.

Past Research Fellowships & Projects

  • **Harvard Medical School (Research Fellow, 2021-2022):** Characterized cutaneous reactions to COVID-19 vaccines using the international registry (PI: Dr. Esther Freeman).
  • **University of Pennsylvania (Clinical Researcher, 2020-2022):** Analyzed representation of skin of color in educational materials and studied dermatology influencers on social media (PI: Dr. Jules Lipoff).
  • **UCSF Breast Care Center (Clinical Researcher, 2016-2017):** Analyzed proton therapy efficacy and genetic factors in radiation response; served in Patient Support Corps and Ask Your Pathologist programs.
  • **Princeton University (Senior Thesis, 2014-2016):** Investigated neural mechanisms of courtship behavior in *Drosophila melanogaster* using optogenetics (PI: Dr. Mala Murthy).
  • **Naguru Teenage Information and Health Centre, Uganda (2018):** Conducted hearing screens for adolescents.
