Ramie Fathy, MD

Dermatology Resident | Researcher | Writer

Welcome! I am currently a PGY-3 Dermatology Resident Physician at Johns Hopkins University. Originally from Knoxville, TN, I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Princeton University (Molecular Biology) and earned my MD from the Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania, where I was inducted into AOA. This site highlights my clinical work, research, leadership, writing, and other interests.

Clinical Experience & Interests

Dermatology Residency (PGY-3)

I am currently completing my dermatology residency training (2023-Present) at Johns Hopkins Hospital, focusing on developing expertise in diagnosing and managing a wide range of skin conditions. My preliminary internship year (2022-2023) was completed at Pennsylvania Hospital.

Clinical Interests

My interests within dermatology include medical dermatology, epidemiology, the impact of environmental factors like climate change on skin health, teledermatology, applications of AI in clinical practice, and exploring integrative approaches to patient care. I am an active member of the Rheumatology-Dermatology Society, Medical Dermatology Society, and Society of Dermatology Hospitalists.
